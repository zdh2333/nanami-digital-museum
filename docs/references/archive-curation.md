# Nanami archive curation record

This record intentionally uses neutral archive IDs and verified capture dates only. It contains no Photos filenames, UUIDs, paths, locations, device information, GPS data, or person identifiers.

## Privacy review

All 16 published photo masters passed both stages below before inclusion:

1. Library metadata screen: the asset belonged to the Nanami pet cluster, was a local still image, and had no detected people scene. This was only a prefilter, never privacy proof.
2. Enlarged visual review: no full or partial human face, reflection, screen face, conversation screenshot, address/location clue, licence plate, document, name/account information, or human body part dominating the image was visible.

The visual review was authoritative. Candidates were rejected when a partial human face, screen, packaging text, person-dominated composition, skin-toned edge detail, hair/clothing, or ambiguous body-shaped background was visible even if the metadata screen had passed. A stricter second review removed three previously approved images and their two derived memes rather than cropping or interpreting the ambiguity. The 6 published memes use only the final approved neutral photo masters and add original text locally.
